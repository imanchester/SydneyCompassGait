------------------- The Sydney Compass-Gait Robot: -------------------------
--- Design and Modeling of an Open Platform for Dynamic Walking Research ---
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
----------------------------------------------------------------------------


This folder contains the CAD files (.zip) of the Sydney Compass Gait walker.
Download and extract the files in a local folder in your computer. 
Open "CompassWalker - HipAssembly.SLDASM" to see the whole assembly of the 
walker. 


Requirements: 
 - SolidWorks 2015 (or above)
